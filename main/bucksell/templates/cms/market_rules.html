Trust and Safety

Real people and money are involved in the transactions of BuckSell marketplace; therefore, it’s essential to protect customers’ interests and use common sense.

Payment

The main method for online monetary transactions will be through the PayPal system, given its strong reputation as a fast, reliable, and sage service. For buyers, please don’t pay until you receive the item and make sure that the item or service is what you asked for and it is complete. Once you click “Pay” or hand over cash, there might not be a change to have your money back, so please be careful when you make your payments! For sellers, please make sure you get paid on the right spot. When you are paid though PayPal on BuckSell.com, wait for the buyers to press “Pay” and wait to receive an email informing the payment transaction. We do not recommend accepting checks, money orders or other methods of payment due to risks of fraud. PayPal via BuckSell.com or cash would be the best Payment methods.

Customer Safety

  • Always meet on your campus or other public spaces and avoid doing large cash transactions.
  • Be careful with your personal information (e.g. your name, address, phone number, campus email, etc.) BuckSell.com allows customers to communicate directly with buyers and sellers, so there will be no need to share your personal information with others.
  • Only accept payment by PayPal via BuckSell.com or in cash. Don’t accept checks, money orders, or other methods of payment.

Dispute Resolution

Because transactions take place between you and other person and BuckSell.com assumes you and others will behave under the federal and local law, BuckSell will not be able to mediate disagreements or issue refunds under any circumstances. If you feel you have been deceived by any means, please contact your local law office for help. It is extremely important for BuckSell.com to keep its transactions and marketplace secure, so please provide us with a copy of the police report by contacting us through support@bucksell.edu so we may take actions to improve our platform.
